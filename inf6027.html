INF6027 Introduction to Data Science

Introduction

This project investigates how weekly trading volume and price movements influence stock price volatility in the health and technology sectors.

Research Questions

  • How do weekly trading volume and price movements impact stock price volatility?
  • What patterns are observable in the health vs. technology sectors?

Key Findings

Detailed analysis revealed that trading volume significantly correlates with price volatility in the health sector, while the technology sector showed stronger correlation patterns during market trends.
